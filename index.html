Team up

In the distant future, humanity is thrown into chaos. Robots, designed to do all the tasks humans no longer want to do, begin to seemingly malfunction instead now ignoring their creators, leaving humanity helpless and unable to fend for themselves.

Amongst the chaos, a rag-tag team of unlikely heroes step up to save humanity.

Armed with their respective skills – and big hammers – the team will bash their way to a solution to help restore the humans’ robots.

Bash Bots

  • Up to 4 players online and local coop – full single player experience
  • Build turrets, use gadgets and specials
  • Travel the futuristic world through vibrant and varied levels
  • Battle against different level mechanics and robots
